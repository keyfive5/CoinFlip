# Privacy Policy — Coin Flip

**Effective date:** July 10, 2026

Coin Flip does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

- **No data collection.** The app has no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no third-party SDKs that collect data.
- **No account.** There is nothing to sign up for.
- **No network access.** The app works entirely offline. Nothing you do in the app ever leaves your device.
- **Local storage only.** Your heads/tails tally is stored on your device only, and is deleted when you delete the app.
